Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about LinkUp.

What is LinkUp?

LinkUp is a social app that helps you discover events, join tables with other people, and split costs for memorable nightlife experiences. It's the easiest way to meet new people and enjoy events together.

How do I join a table?

Simply browse events near you, find one you're interested in, and tap "Join Table". You can see who else is going, the cost split, and all the event details before confirming.

Is LinkUp free to use?

Yes! The LinkUp app is completely free to download and use. You only pay for the events you choose to attend, and costs are transparently split with your table.

How does cost splitting work?

When you join a table, the total cost is automatically divided among all participants. You can see exactly how much you'll pay before confirming. Payments are processed securely through the app.

Can I host my own events?

Absolutely! Any user can create and host events on LinkUp. Share your table at your favorite venue and invite others to join. It's a great way to meet new people.

What cities is LinkUp available in?

LinkUp is currently available in major cities including Miami, New York, Los Angeles, London, and more. We're expanding to new cities every month.

How do I contact support?

You can reach our support team through the app or by emailing info@linkupapp.com. We typically respond within 24 hours.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and secure payment processing. Your payment details are never stored on our servers.
